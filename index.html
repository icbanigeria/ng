About ICBAN

The Institute of Certified Corporate & Commercial Business Administrators of Nigeria was established and incorporated as a professional association of business administrators at all levels of certification, competence, proficiency and professional sophistication spanning both the private and public sectors of Nigeria's national economy. It is a not-for-profit professional body for business administrators. The Institute has a growing national presence with members employed in all sectors of the economy and makes significant contribution to the efficiency of every business at all levels. We are also the officially recognised professional body for graduates of business administration in Nigeria.


It was established to be a professional clearing house and certification hub for all who are and will preside or function in business management positions whether at the lower, intermediate, management or executive cadres of our business organisations.

*The complexities of modern day business management and administration models globally has necessitated that every business manager or anyone in the business management value chain must not only be knowledgeable but must be constantly updated, appreciating and developing with cutting edge capacities that will ensure his relevance in today's fast-paced intellectual globalisation and unabating techno-social innovations.Tactical and strategic business skills and models are now global citizens that could not be allowed to remain the exclusive companions and preserves of certain racial demographies.

*The 21st century will no doubt witness development in all spheres of human endeavours especially in the business and corporate world with young corporations taking quantum leaps to upstage hitherto leading world business concerns within unimaginably short period of time.It is therefore a pressing and compelling necessity that businesses and business administrators especially in this corner of the globe must stay on top of their critical business knowledge and information game to keep pace with the rest of the business world.

*ICBAN is dedicated to empowering business professionals at all levels to drive this initiative and performance.Our members are highly prized professionals and business administrators who are well trained and fortified to deliver professional competence and excellence in all business sectors.

*As the leading professional body for those working in the business administrative and management sectors of business enterprises and organisations,we offer qualifications and membership grades to suit all potential members.

*The ICBAN designation represents high ethical and professional standards and a commitment to raising the profile of the business administration profession in Nigeria.
